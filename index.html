The Methodology

The Voice-First Framework

Your Complete Guide to Building a Category-of-One Brand

Before You Start

This framework takes 90 minutes to 3 hours to complete.

  • You'll need: A quiet space, honesty, and time.
  • Three rules: Don't rush. Don't skip sections. Don't perform.

The goal is TRUTH, not performance.

The Formula

The Voice-First Framework is a systematic process for discovering what makes you uniquely valuable.

VOICE Who You Are (Belief + Process + Experience)
↓
EXPRESSION How You Show It (Stories + Expertise + Vision)
↓
CATEGORY OF ONE The Result

Phase 1: Voice (The Excavation)

Discover your unique intersection before you build.

Step 1: Discover Your Core Belief

Your Core Belief is the ONE contrarian truth you hold about your field that most people get wrong. It is your philosophical differentiator.

Excavation Questions

Format: "I believe [YOUR BELIEF], not [COMMON BELIEF]."

Step 2: Identify Your Core Process

Your Core Process is the natural, repeatable sequence your brain uses to solve problems. It is your methodological differentiator.

Common Patterns: The Excavator (Dig → Uncover → Build), The Simplifier (Observe → Remove → Clarify), The Builder (Identify → Design → Scale).

Format: [Verb 1] → [Verb 2] → [Verb 3] → [Verb 4]

Step 3: Extract Your Core Experience

Your lived experience is your authority. You don't need credentials; you need to own your journey.

Step 4: The Voice Statement

Synthesize everything into one paragraph. This is your filter.

Template: I believe [CORE BELIEF]. I help [WHO] by [CORE PROCESS], so they [OUTCOME]. I'm credible because [CORE EXPERIENCE].

Phase 3: Positioning

Translating your Voice into a clear market position.

Step 8: One-Line Positioning

Formula: I help [WHO] who [SPECIFIC PROBLEM] [YOUR METHOD] so they [SPECIFIC OUTCOME].

Step 9: The 30-Second Pitch

What you say when people ask "Tell me more."

Template: [One-Liner]. Here's the problem: [The Pain]. Most people [Common Approach]. I do it differently: [Voice-Filtered Method]. [Result].
