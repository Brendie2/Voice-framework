THE VOICE-FIRST FRAMEWORK

Your Complete Guide to Building a Category-of-One Brand

BEFORE YOU START

This framework takes 90 minutes to 3 hours to complete.

You'll need: A quiet space, honesty with yourself.

Three rules:

  1. Don't rush
  2. Don't skip sections
  3. Don't try to sound impressive

The goal is TRUTH, not performance.

THE COMPLETE FORMULA

Your Voice is WHO you are. Your Expression is HOW you show it. When expressed consistently, you become a category-of-one brand.

VOICE (Who You Are) Core Belief + Core Process + Core Experience
↓
EXPRESSION (How You Show It) Stories + Expertise + Vision
(Filtered through your Voice)
↓
RESULT Category-of-One Brand

PHASE 1: VOICE (The Excavation)

STEP 1: DISCOVER YOUR CORE BELIEF

Your Core Belief is the ONE contrarian truth you hold about your field that most people get wrong.

Question 1: What advice in your industry makes you angry?
Question 2: What do you see people doing that doesn't work?
Question 3: If you could change ONE thing about how your topic is taught, what would it be?

Your Core Process is the natural, repeatable sequence your brain uses to solve problems.
